=== Getting Started ===::1
The first part of this book provides some heavily recommended objectives you should try and accomplish as soon as possible.  This is a "kitchen sink" type pack, so you are free to do as you wish, but your options are rather limited in the early game and you will really want these basic objectives accomplished before attempting any of the other content::2
Review the very helpful FTB Utils guide (in your inventory screen)::0
Use your crystal ball and choose "I live alone" and claim your free house::0
